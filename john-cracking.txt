John the Ripper (JTR) Quick Reference
====================================

1. Basic Automated Cracking Mode:
john password_hashes.txt

2. Wordlist / Dictionary Mode:
john --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt password_hashes.txt

3. Check Current Session Progress:
Press [Spacebar] or [Enter] while running, or type:
john --status

4. Show Successfully Cracked Passwords:
john --show password_hashes.txt

5. Target Specific Hash Formats:
john --format=raw-md5 hashes.txt
